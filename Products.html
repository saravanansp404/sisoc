Smart Energy Meter

Presently DISCOMs in India use Manual meters at consumers' premises for billing.
Major drawbacks:
1) Too many lineman's required to read the meter of each consumer.
2) Chances of error prone due to manual intervention is more
3) Difficulty during critical times like NOW - Pandemic time.
Shortage of employees/restrictions create a big backlog in measuring the readings.

Solution : Smart Energy Meters ?
No doubt Govt push a lot for completing installation of 25 crores Smart Meters in next 3 years. Smart metering is a good solution but few questions are to be answered Installation of Smart Meters will cost Billions of investment. How if this investment is not satisfactory as expected? The disposal of currently existing millions of meters (almost) A question that can't be answered.
